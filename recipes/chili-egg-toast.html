Chili Egg on Toast

Description

Chili oil scrambled eggs is a breakfast dish that adds a little spice to a classic staple.

It is quick to make and can most likely be made with ingredients already in your home. 2 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Slices of your preffered bread
  • 4 Tbsp chili oil
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • Salt
  • Optional: Butter

Steps

  1. Scoop out the inside of your half-avocado and smash into a spreadable paste
  2. Crack open an egg into a small bowl and whisk till scrambled. Add about 5 dashes of salt.
  3. Heat a pan on high heat then add 2 tbsp of chili oil.
  4. Once the chili oil is bubbling, add your egg. Then turn off your heat. The residual heat will cook the egg.
  5. Heat a seperate pan to medium heat to toast your bread. Add about 1/2 a tbsp of butter if you desire.
  6. Remove egg from first pan.
  7. Spread your avocado spread onto toast then lay egg on top.
  8. Repeat steps 2-7 for second egg.
