Bang Bang Sauce

Description

This is a spicy-sweet sauce that you can pair with a multitude of dishes, such as shrimp.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of mayo
  • 2 Tbsp sweet chili sauce
  • 1 Tbsp sriracha
  • 1 Tsp rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Stir mayo, chili sauce, sriracha, and rice vinegar together in a small bowl.
