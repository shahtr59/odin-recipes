Hot Wings

Description

This is an easy chicken wing recipe that can be prepared in an air fryer.

A wing sauce recipe is also included but you can use your own if you feel.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lbs. of chicken wings
  • Baking powder
  • 2 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp black pepper
